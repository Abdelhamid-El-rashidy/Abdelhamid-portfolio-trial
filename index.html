About Me

who am I?

I'm Abdelhamid Ahmed, an 17-year-old Egyptian based in Giza, a self-taught front-end web developer, and a rising junior at STEM High School for Boys - 6th of October. I intend to major in computer science, with a focus on Web development.


In 2018, Interestingly, I got introduced to programming for the first time through a YouTube video. I began exploring all of the technical subfields of computer science before deciding that web development would be my career.


Since I believe that people should constantly strive to improve their professional skills and learn new things in addition to their academic life, I started learning and practicing web development technologies, which helped me create this website through which I can share my accomplishments and experiences in a variety of areas of my life with you.
